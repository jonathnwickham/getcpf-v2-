Do Tourists Need a CPF in Brazil?

Technically, no. You can survive a short trip without one. But after a few days of paying international card fees, not being able to order on iFood, using hotel Wi-Fi because you cannot get a local SIM, and paying gringo prices because you cannot use Pix, you will wish you had one.

What changes with a CPF

Without CPF

  • International roaming or cafe Wi-Fi
  • Foreign card fees on every purchase
  • No iFood, Rappi, or food delivery
  • Cannot shop online in Brazil
  • Cash only at many places
  • Paying tourist prices everywhere

With CPF

  • Local SIM with data (R$30/month)
  • Nubank account with no fees
  • iFood, Rappi, Uber Eats
  • Amazon BR, Mercado Livre
  • Pix payments everywhere
  • Local prices, local access

Yes, tourists can get a CPF

There is no residency requirement. No work visa needed. If you have a passport and can show up at a Correios office, you can get a CPF. The process takes about 15 minutes and costs R$7 (about $1.50).

And here is the best part: a CPF never expires. Get one on your first trip and it is ready for every future visit. Think of it as a one-time investment in a better Brazil experience.

The address problem for tourists

The CPF application requires a Brazilian address. As a tourist, you probably do not have a utility bill or rental contract. Your options:

  • ✓ Host declaration letter from whoever you are staying with (friend, Airbnb host, relative). This is the most reliable option.
  • ~ Hotel booking confirmation. Some offices accept this, some do not. Risky to rely on.
  • × No address proof at all. You will be turned away.

Frequently asked questions

Can tourists get a CPF?

Yes. No residency or special visa required. Visit a Correios with your passport and address proof.

Is it worth it for a short trip?

If staying more than a few days, yes. Local SIM, food delivery, Pix payments, and online shopping all require it. Plus it never expires.

What address do I use in a hotel?

Hotels are unreliable as address proof. A host declaration letter is better. GET CPF generates one for you.

How long does it take?

About 15 minutes. Often same-day at Correios.
